INFORMATION FOR PROSPECTIVE MEMBERS

The Mixers are the most well attended events of the year, typically consisting of 12-24 enthusiastic tennis players. The Saturday monthly mixers run in the March-October time frame. No reservations are needed, you just need to show up. $5 for SCTC members, $10 for non-members. If you are interested in playing USTA Leagues, the club hosts USTA teams each season. Those playing on SCTC USTA teams must be club members. Contact each USTA captain for the team you wish to join. Also, you may contact the membership chair or the USTA rep for more information.

SCTC MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

The Santa Clara Tennis Club is a Community Tennis Association (CTA) sponsored by the city of Santa Clara for the purpose of fostering tennis participation in Santa Clara. Club memberships run January through December and may be renewed yearly. Yearly membership benefits include:

  • Monthly newsletter and membership roster
  • Monthly mixers (social tennis for all levels), March through October
  • Tennis clinics to improve your game, free to club members
  • Annual Bob Hughes Memorial Pig-Out (tennis and barbecue)
  • Special Mixers which include Cinco de Mayo celebration with lunch from Tostadas in May and a social with ice cream from Rocko's in August. Some events may include live music.
  • USTA Teams
  • Annual End of the Year Tennis Dinner in October.

The July Pig-Out, special events and year-end banquet are all partially subsidized by the club, keeping prices low for members.

Please fill out the online membership form. Check the online membership roster if you aren't sure whether your membership is current. SCTC membership is required to play on club USTA teams.
