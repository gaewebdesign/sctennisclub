Thursday October 19 Year end Dinner

 

Welcome to the Santa Clara Tennis Club. The club offers several opportunities for tennis play. Activities include monthly Friday clinics, Saturday tennis socials and USTA league teams. Some of the socials are specials -in addition to great tennis we have catered food from local restaurants, including lunch from JC's Barbecue for the annual July Pig-out. In previous years, we had barbecue from Armadillo Willys and The Smoking Pig.

To celebrate Cinco de Mayo we feasted on birria tacos/rice/beans from Tostadas . To cool down during a hot August we had a speical tennis social with ice cream from Rocko's in Santa Clara! We are the only tennis club to have live music at our events. The July Pigout barbecue featured Margie Wong of Going Vertical Music .
