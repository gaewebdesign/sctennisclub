Welcome to the Santa Clara Tennis Club. The club offers several opportunities for tennis play. Activities include monthly Friday clinics, Saturday tennis socials and USTA league teams. Some of the socials are specials - offering great food from local restaurants, including a catered lunch from JC's Barbecue for the annual July Pig-out. In previous years, we had barbecue from Armadillo Willys and the Smoking Pig.

To celebrate Cinco de Mayo we had lunch from Tostadas . To cool down during a hot August we had a ice cream social! We are the only tennis club to have live music at our events. The July Pigout barbecue featured Margie Wong of Going Vertical Music .
